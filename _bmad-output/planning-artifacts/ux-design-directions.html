Step 9 · Design Direction Mockups

Six directions for a calm, polished, trust-first personal site.

Each direction uses the same palette, spacing logic, and core interaction principles, but shifts the layout, density, emphasis, and motion character. The goal is not to pick a final UI pixel-for-pixel, but to discover which visual language feels most like your site.

How to evaluate
  • Which version makes identity and qualifications easiest to grasp in seconds?
  • Which one feels most custom without becoming fussy?
  • Where does motion feel supportive rather than decorative?
  • Which balance of warmth, professionalism, and proof feels most like you?
